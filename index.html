Step 1 – Executive Summary

Business Concept

R2 Raffle Club is a mobile and web-based gaming platform designed to attract Betway and Hollywoodbets users by offering a fun, voucher-only prize system. Players deposit any amount starting from R2, spin a prize wheel with closed mystery boxes, and stand a chance to win Betway or Hollywoodbets voucher codes instantly.

How It Works

  • Users pick their spin amount.
  • The higher the spin amount, the higher the potential voucher value hidden in the boxes.
  • Players may win more, win less, or land on an empty box.
  • Vouchers won appear instantly in the player’s wallet as a balance.
  • Voucher numbers are sent only upon withdrawal by email or SMS (partial withdrawals allowed).

Key Features

  • Voucher-only payouts – No cash withdrawals; winnings are always vouchers.
  • Instant prize reveal after spin by clicking the chosen mystery box.
  • Scalable prizes based on spin amount.

Revenue Model

Revenue comes from the house edge—users often win less than their bet or empty boxes.
Example: 4,000 daily spins, only ~10% are big winners; rest generate profit.

Target Market

  • South African Betway & Hollywoodbets users.
  • Gamblers seeking low-cost, high-reward spins.
  • Casual gamers interested in vouchers.
